Why Government Contracts
The most overlooked revenue
channel for small businesses.

Most of your competitors are not bidding. That's your advantage — right now, before the market in your area gets crowded.

Set-asides cut your competition

Contracts reserved for small businesses exclude large corporations entirely. You're competing against companies your size — not billion-dollar players.

Government always pays

Fixed Net-30 or Net-60 payment terms on every contract. No chasing invoices, no clients going silent, no bad debt. Predictable cash flow.

Contracts renew for years

Win one $300K landscaping contract, renew it 5 years = $1.5M from a single bid. Government contracts are multi-year by design.

Agencies need to find you

Federal agencies have legally mandated small business spending targets. Contracting officers are actively searching for qualified small vendors.

Build a compounding reputation

Past performance is the #1 factor in winning future contracts. One win leads to renewals, extensions, and referrals to other contracting officers.

Certifications open exclusive lanes

Veteran-owned, woman-owned, minority-owned, and 8(a) certifications unlock contracts with near-zero competition. Free to apply for.
