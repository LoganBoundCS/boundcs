Why Government Contracts
The most overlooked revenue channel for small businesses.

Most of your competitors aren't bidding. The money goes to whoever shows up.

Set-asides cut your competition

Contracts reserved for small businesses legally exclude every large corporation. You compete against companies your own size.

Government always pays — on time

Fixed Net-30 or Net-60 on every contract. No chasing invoices. No bad debt. Predictable cash flow you can build a business around.

One win compounds into five more

Win a contract, perform well, and renewals become automatic. Win a $300K contract, renew it 5 years — that's $1.5M from one bid.

Certifications unlock exclusive lanes

Veteran-owned, woman-owned, HUBZone, 8(a) — these unlock contracts with near-zero competition. Free to apply for.

Agencies are required to find you

Federal agencies have legally mandated small business spending targets. Contracting officers actively hunt for qualified small vendors.

Steady work, guaranteed revenue

Whatever your trade is, there are new bids posted daily that most contractors in your area never even see. That's money sitting on the table for whoever shows up.
