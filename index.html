Why Government Contracts
The most overlooked revenue
channel for small businesses.

Most of your competitors aren't bidding. That gap is your opportunity — right now, before it closes.

Set-asides cut your competition

Contracts reserved for small businesses legally exclude large corporations. You compete against companies your size — not billion-dollar players with 50-person BD teams.

Government always pays on time

Fixed Net-30 or Net-60 on every contract. No chasing invoices, no clients going silent, no bad debt. Predictable cash flow you can actually plan around.

Contracts compound over time

Win one contract, perform well, and renewals become easy. Past performance is the single biggest factor in winning future bids. One win leads to five more.

Certifications open exclusive doors

Veteran-owned, woman-owned, minority-owned, and 8(a) certifications unlock contracts with near-zero competition. Free to apply for — most contractors never bother.

Agencies are required to find you

Federal agencies have legally mandated small business spending targets. Contracting officers are actively hunting for qualified small vendors to meet those goals.
